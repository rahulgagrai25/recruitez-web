About Recruitez

We're revolutionizing the recruitment industry through AI-powered technology and specialized training programs that connect top talent with dream opportunities.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Recruitez emerged from a simple observation: the recruitment process was broken for both employers and job seekers. Companies struggled to find qualified candidates, while talented individuals couldn't connect with the right opportunities.

Our founders, with backgrounds in HR technology and AI, set out to create a platform that would transform this experience. By leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms, we've developed a system that accurately matches candidates with positions where they'll truly excel.

Today, Recruitez serves thousands of companies and candidates across multiple industries, with a special focus on helping college students bridge the skills gap through our Launchpad program.
