Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate will be applied immediately. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all our monthly plans are subscription-based with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time. We also offer annual plans with a 15% discount for those who prefer to commit longer-term.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special discounted pricing for registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with proof of your status to learn more about our nonprofit and education discounts.

What happens if I exceed my monthly candidate profile limits?

If you approach your monthly limit, we'll send you a notification. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional candidate profile packs. Unused profiles do not roll over to the next month.
